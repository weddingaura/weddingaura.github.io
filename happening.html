Wedding Schedule
23rd OCTOBER , 2018

The Arrival Breakfast

After you check-in and settle down in your rooms at Lariya resort/Garh Govind Resort, join us for breakfast at 10:00am , ONYX Restaurant of Lariya Resort.

Mayra

After breakfast , we will assemble at Samagama Banquet Hall ,1st floor of Lariya Resort for Mayra function at 12:15pm Hosted by Smt. Shakuntla Satsangi , Anand Prakash Satsangi & family.

Lunch

Mayra function will be followed by traditional Rajasthani lunch in the same Samagama Banquet Hall at 2:00pm

Sangeet Sandhya & Ring Ceremony

Time to assemble for Sangeet Sandhya at Lariya Lawn is 7:30pm. After Geet-Sangeet and dance there will be Ring ceremony at same lawn area followed by dinner. Dance , drink and dine to celebrate the occassion.

24th OCTOBER , 2018

Bane Vinayak Pooja

We will all meet for the Bane Vinayak Muhurat/Ganesh Poojan ceremony , 10:00am at AGANTUKA HALL ,1st floor of Lariya resort which will be followed by breakfast at the adjoining banquet hall.

Mehndi

A team of expert will be present from 11:00am for putting some beautiful design of Mehndi on the hands of beautiful ladies.

Haldi

The haldi ceremony which is a pre-wedding ritual where haldi paste will be applied on our dear bride Shubhangi and bless her as she gears up for her big day along with lots of music , fun and masti. The ceremony will be at Samagama Hall , 11:30am. The dress code for the function will be yellow.

Vinayak Bhoj

All relatives and friends will assemble for lunch at Samagama Banquet Hall , 2:00pm onwards.

Baraat Reception

We all will assemble at the lush green garden area of Lariya Resort at 8:00pm to welcome baratis. Enjoy Dinner thereafter with variety of food which will be followed by Pheras and other related functions at the Lawn area in between the two building blocks of Lariya.

SAYONARA Breakfast

Kindly join us for breakfast at ONYX restaurant of Lariya resort before leaving the place and saying goodbye.

Thank you

Hope you will enjoy the wedding ceremony and we will try our best to make the event comfortable and memorable for you all.
